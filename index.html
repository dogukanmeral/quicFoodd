Ice Soup

Ingredients

Fresh water

Well-seasoned ice

Minerals

Blue food paint(Optional)

Ocean salt

Steps

Preparation

As the main element, get them big old ice cubes frozen. You can use pre-frozen cubes or freeze them yourself which is prefered. Use high mineral water to maximise the taste and also general health. Let them settle in freezer so they can become more crispy.

The Holy Water

Now let's back to fundamentals, get our base ready. Get your high mineral water and pour it into a bowl. Let it settle for a few minutes. After that put our blue food paint to get that beautiful Aqua effect.

Make it more authentic

To create that ocean effect, we gotta bring out the whole ocean. Let's put some ocean salt in our mix but be careful, we have to find the sweet spot; not too salty but also not too sweet.

Presentation

We have our beautiful soup ready. To make it more thirsty use color code light blue. A blue bowl is always preffered. Be the unorthodox and break the rules: Instead of using a spoon, serve the soup with a straw, use mini umbrellas and other sort of decorations to create island effect. Enjoy it ;)
